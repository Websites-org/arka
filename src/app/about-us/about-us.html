ABOUT ARKA INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Jnanambika Educational Trust is founded by Dr Venugopal in to nurture young minds, retain their creative talents and at the 2018, the Trust has a vision to drive Arka International School, same time chisel every child to be competent, and happy to serve the national goals. With more than 40+ years of rich experience in different government as well as private academic institutions, Dr. Venugopal has embarked upon a journey to create and sustain Jnanambika Trust which would result in the advent of Arka International School near Kolar, to provide best service in the field of education. With a subtle blend of the modern and the traditional, Arka International School will be a different school that would adopt Play Way method and DIY tools (Do It Yourself). Sustaining the creative best in every child, Arka would build life-skills, scientific approach and lateral thinking skills in every child by making learning a fun activity Jnanambika Educational trust is committed to provide high quality learning experience to every child by adopting time tested tactics, customized learning strategies based on the learning abilities of a child, with inputs from highly qualified teachers, well provided infrastructure and highly evolved Library and ICT services. Arka International School as an international school in its standards and would draw lessons from Indian wisdom in the field of education.

A Message from the Principal: Our School Curriculum

Dear Parents, Students, and Community Members,

I am excited to share with you the comprehensive curriculum we have developed for our students. Our educational program is designed to nurture young minds, foster creativity, and prepare our children for future success.

We believe education extends far beyond traditional subjects. Our “Arts” program allows students to express themselves through visual arts, music, and drama. “Physical Education” ensures our students develop healthy habits and understand the importance of physical fitness and teamwork.

Our dedicated teachers use a variety of instructional methods to accommodate different learning styles. We believe every child learns differently, and our curriculum reflects this understanding through differentiated instruction, collaborative learning opportunities, and individualized support when needed.

We emphasize critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration — skills that will serve our students well in an ever-changing world. Our teachers regularly participate in professional development to ensure they are using the most effective, research-based teaching strategies.

Mrs. Keerthy L
Principal

We are committed to meeting each student where they are and helping them reach their full potential. Our curriculum includes support for students who need additional help as well as enrichment opportunities for those ready for greater challenges.

# Partnership with Families

Your involvement in your child’s education is invaluable. We encourage you to ask your children about what they’re learning, support their homework efforts, and maintain open communication with their teachers. Together, we can ensure every student has the opportunity to thrive.

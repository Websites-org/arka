Co-Curricular Activities - Learning beyond the classroom

At Arka International School, education goes beyond textbooks. We believe in nurturing the mind, body, and spirit through meaningful co-curricular activities that support a child’s overall development.

Through a balance of structured academics and hands-on experiences, students develop creativity, discipline, leadership and confidence.

Karate

Our karate program builds self-discipline, focus, and physical fitness. Students learn important values like respect, resilience, and self-control while gaining practical self-defense skills in a structured and safe environment.

Dance

Dance sessions bring rhythm, joy and expression into the school day. Children explore various styles of movement that improve body coordination, confidence and stage presence – while also celebrating culture and creativity.

Scouts & Guides

The Scout & Guide program develops leadership, teamwork, responsibility and service-mindedness. Through camps, group tasks, and community activities, students grow into socially responsible and self-reliant individuals.

Art & Craft

Our art and craft classes help children express themselves visually and build fine motor skills. From sketching and coloring to working with materials, students explore creativity with freedom and imagination.

Why Co-Curricular Matters

These activities are not just add-ons – they are a vital part of the school experience. They shape character, improve communication and help students discover their interests and talents outside academics.
